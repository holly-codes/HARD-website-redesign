How To Donate to HARD

Due to COVID-19, we, like all other derby leagues, aren't able to fundraise or have bouts as usual, to help subsidize our season and other expenses. If you want to donate to the league, you can find the information here about how to do that.

Donation Information

We know that things right now are hard for everyone, and we are no exception. If you are able, we are immensely appreciative of any amount that you would be willing to donate. Any amount helps us to ensure that we can come back after it is safe to do so, and that we can continue to put on bouts containing all of the heart-stopping action you've come to know and love.

You can submit monetary donations to us via PayPal by searching @hartfordrollerderby. If you have any questions, please feel free to email our treasurer, at HARDTreasury@gmail.com.

Again, thank you for your generosity. If you cannot support us monetarily, we are always thankful for any material, social, emotional, or other support. Thank you for being in our community. We love you and cannot wait to see you at our bouts!
