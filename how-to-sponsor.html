How To Sponsor HARD

Interested in sponsoring us, but not sure what that means or how to do it? We've got some useful information below.

Sponsorship Information

HARD is always accepting sponsors of any type, whether it be sponsoring our teams for our 2019 season, donating a prize for one of our raffles, or any one of many contribution types. If you're interested in sponsoring us, please get in touch. We've got many sponsorship tiers, and would love to find which one would best suit your means.

We are also always accepting donations for prizes for the raffles that we hold at our bouts. This is another fantastic way to get involved, support HARD, and get some advertisement for your business!

Benefits of sponsorship include:

  • An ad in the programs at our bouts
  • Mentions on our social media
  • Signage at home bouts
  • Cross-promotional events
  • ...and others, depending on your sponsorship tier level!

Ready to get involved? Let's chat!

Contact Leah Simmons (a.k.a. Whiskey Business) at Sponsorship@HartfordAreaRollerDerby.com to get started!
