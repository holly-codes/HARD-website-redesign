We Are Hartford

We are Hartford Area Roller Derby (HARD), the premier women's roller derby league in central Connecticut.

Our Mission Statement

HARD is dedicated to promoting women's flat track roller derby in the Greater Hartford, Connecticut area. As a 501c3 non-profit organization, HARD is organized for charitable purposes and to foster interest in the sport of roller derby. This includes building roller skating skills, creating a local roller derby community, establishing positive relationships within the community, and promoting teamwork, respect, and empowerment of women.

Team Rosters - Coming Soon!

HARD consists of two teams - our WFTDA-chartered Hartford Wailers, and our Beat City Bedrockers. Together, they comprise over 30 skaters. We will be posting our rosters soon - keep an eye out for those!
